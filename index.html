About

Welcome to the professional webpage of Subhajit Ghosh, a dedicated researcher in computational biology. With over five years of extensive experience in computational biology, bioinformatics, and molecular modeling, Subhajit's research focuses on integrating network biology, transcriptomics, and machine learning approaches to uncover novel biomarkers and therapeutic insights for complex diseases such as hepatocellular carcinoma, polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS), and rheumatoid arthritis.

His expertise spans statistical data analysis, molecular docking, molecular dynamics simulations, and development of advanced Python and R scripts tailored for genomics and clinical data interpretation.
